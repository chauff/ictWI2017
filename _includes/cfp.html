Call for Case Studies

To make ICT with Industry 2017 a success, we seek challenging industrially relevant case studies on ICT topics, applications and tools. You are invited to submit a proposal for a case study in collaboration with large companies and/or SMEs.

Benefits for applying companies: the workshop offers you one week to collaborate with a team of 6-10 dedicated researchers from various ICT-disciplines to tackle the problems in your proposed case. The goal is to come to a practical solution or new horizons with new insights.

Benefits for applying researchers: the workshop offers you one week to explore your research ideas in collaboration with your company partners. You will have a team of 6-10 dedicated researchers from a various ICT-disciplines to work with. The goal is to come to concrete ideas which can be used for e.g. scientific papers and funding proposals to be submitted to NWO or Horizon 2020 calls.

Selection criteria: five challenges will be selected by the organizing committee, with leading experts from different fields of ICT according to their:

  1. Is the challenge scientifically interesting, particularly from an ICT point of view?
  2. Is the challenge urgent?
  3. Is it likely that a solution or significant steps towards a solution to the challenge can be obtained within the timeframe of one week?
  4. Can you commit to your involvement during the workshop?

The full call for case studies is available in PDF format. The call overview also contains the application form which should be send to ictwithindustry@nwo.nl/.
