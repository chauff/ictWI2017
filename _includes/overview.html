Workshop Setup

The ICT with Industry workshop brings together scientists, in particular (junior) research staff and PhDâ€™s, and professionals from industry and governments. The workshop revolves around a number of concrete case studies, which are subject to an intense week of analyzing, discussing, modeling and accelerating solutions. The 2017 edition is the fifth in the series: past editions were held in 2016, 2015, 2014 and 2013. Past industry projects have come among others from KLM, Beeld en Geluid, Thales, ING, Greenclouds and IBM.

Workshop Objectives

1. To stimulate contact between ICT research and industrial R&D, by identifying and analyzing challenging problems in multidisciplinary teams.
2. To obtain creative solutions for industrial problems.
3. To give insight into the wide range of possibilities ICT research offers , and thereby enable accelerated innovation.
4. To enrich the PhD students' and postdocs' experience in collaborating with industry.
